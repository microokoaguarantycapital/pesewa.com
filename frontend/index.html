How Pesewa.com Works

1

Join or Create a Group

Create groups with people you know or join existing ones through referrals. Max 1000 members per group, minimum 5.

2

Request or Offer Loans

Borrowers request specific utility loans. Lenders offer loans with 10% weekly interest. Disbursement within 10 minutes.

3

Cross-Group Flexibility

Borrow from one group to repay another if needed. Be part of up to 4 groups simultaneously with good rating.

4

Repay & Build Trust

Repay within 7 days (daily payments allowed). Build your reputation to access more groups and higher limits.
