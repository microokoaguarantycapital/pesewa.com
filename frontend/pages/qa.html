General Questions

Pesewa.com is a revolutionary peer-to-peer emergency micro-lending platform built on human trust via referrals. We connect people in trusted circles (groups) to lend and borrow small amounts for specific urgent needs like transport, food, medicine, etc.

We focus on human trust (referral-only), specific utility lending (12 categories), community-based groups, and dual roles (everyone can borrow and lend). We don't use algorithmic scoring and all transactions happen off-platform (we provide trust infrastructure only).

We operate in 12 African countries: Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, Uganda, South Africa, Rwanda, Burundi, Somalia, South Sudan, Ethiopia, and Congo. Each country has its own groups and local currency support.

Yes. We use Firebase authentication, encrypted data, and secure APIs. However, note that all financial transactions happen off-platform via M-Pesa, mobile money, bank transfers, etc. We only manage the trust, grouping, and accountability infrastructure.

1. Register on our platform.
2. Join or create a group (referral required).
3. Choose your role (borrower and/or lender).
4. Lenders: Select subscription tier.
5. Start lending or borrowing within your trusted circles.

Groups Questions

Groups are trusted circles of up to 1,000 members. They can be based on family, friends, church, profession, community, etc. Groups are country-locked (no cross-country lending).

You need a referral from an existing member of that group. During registration, you'll provide 2 guarantors from the same group. The group admin approves new members.

Maximum 4 groups per user. This ensures trust density and prevents over-borrowing. You can migrate between groups if you have a good repayment record and a referral.

Yes, any user can create unlimited groups. You become the admin/founder and can invite others. Groups must have between 5 and 1,000 members.
