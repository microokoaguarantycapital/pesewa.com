How Pesewa.com Works

1

Create or Join Groups

Form trusted circles with friends, family, or colleagues. Maximum 1,000 members per group, country-locked for local relevance.

2

Referral-Only Entry

New members join only via referral from existing trusted members. Every member provides 2 guarantors for verification.

3

Dual Role System

Everyone can be both a borrower and a lender. Borrowers pay no subscription; lenders subscribe based on their tier.

4

Emergency Lending

Borrow for 12 specific emergency categories like transport, food, medicine, or school fees. 10% weekly interest, 7-day repayment.

5

Trust-Based Accountability

Lenders maintain personal ledgers. Ratings and blacklist system ensure accountability while preserving human connections.

6

Cross-Group Flexibility

Borrow in one group to repay in another. Maximum 4 groups per user for optimal trust and risk distribution.
